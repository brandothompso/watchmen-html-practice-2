FAQ

How do patrols stay visible without feeling intrusive?

Officers are positioned for a calm, professional presence in lobbies and guest corridors while increasing visibility during late check-in periods, event closeout, and overnight quiet hours.

Can officers coordinate with overnight management teams?

Yes. Incident notes and shift summaries can be relayed to night audit or management with timestamps, locations, and actions taken so teams have clean handoff records.
