FAQ

Can coverage support both business hours and after-close windows?

Yes. Schedules can combine daytime visibility during peak traffic with focused after-close checks around loading areas, service corridors, and shared parking lots.

How are repeated trouble spots tracked over time?

Patrol reporting can tag recurring zones by tenant bay, entrance, or lot section so property teams can spot patterns and coordinate follow-up with tenants.
