FAQ

Can HOA boards receive summary-level reporting?

Yes. Reports can be provided in board-friendly summaries that highlight patrol frequency, observed issues, and trend notes for monthly community meetings.

How are resident-facing interactions documented?

Documentation can include street or amenity location, courtesy-contact details, and escalation outcomes so managers can respond consistently to resident concerns.
