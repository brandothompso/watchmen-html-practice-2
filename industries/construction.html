Industry Operational Challenges

Construction sites face shifting perimeters, changing vendor schedules, and high-value equipment exposure. Coverage must track site evolution while maintaining predictable deterrence and rapid escalation pathways.

Common Pressure Points

  • After-hours visibility gaps across shared access points.
  • Need for credible documentation for managers and ownership groups.
  • Dispatch coordination expectations when incidents escalate.

Recommended Patrol / Service Model

Mobile patrols anchor perimeter and gate checks. On-call response supports alarms or trespass events, while periodic foot checks validate fence lines, laydown yards, and temporary structures.

FAQ

Can coverage adapt as project phases change?

Yes. Patrol cadence can be aligned by day type, seasonality, incident trends, and budget realities while preserving reporting consistency.

How is after-hours activity documented for stakeholders?

Teams receive end-of-shift reporting with timestamps, observations, photos when relevant, and clear escalation notes for follow-up.
